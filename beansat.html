BeanSat - A nanosatellite for crop cultivation monitoring in milligravity

The BeanSat project is a projet invented as part of the Space Mission Planning and Operations (SMPO) course in the Masters of Space Engineering at the Technische Universit√§t Berlin. The paper is a mission idea invented by me, with some project management and planning components added. It concerns the feasability of launching a bio-payload, a small terrarium, on-board a CubeSat being ejected from a REXUS sounding rocket. During the descent, the spacecraft would perform different manoeuvers, in order to prove the viability and efficiency of growing fruit-bearing plants in a milligravity space environment.
